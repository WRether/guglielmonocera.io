Introduction to formal moduli problems (2020-2021, Scuola Normale Superiore and University of Pisa, co-organizer)

This was a reading seminar based on Derived Algebraic Geometry X by Jacob Lurie, and organised by Federico Conti, Julian Demeio, Michele Pernice, Morena Porzio and myself. It ran from December 2020 to July 2021
In this course we introduced the necessary background (starting from basics in deformation theory and ∞-category theory) to understand the link between differentially graded Lie algebras and formal moduli problems, as exposed in Lurie's note, Theorem 2.0.2.
Bibliography: [Calaque-Grivaux] Damien Calaque and Julien Grivaux, Formal moduli problems and formal derived stacks, 2019
